About ElectroPro

Your trusted partner in electrical safety compliance and evaluation services in Kenya.

Our Mission

To ensure electrical safety and compliance across Kenya by providing professional evaluation services and promoting adherence to national and international electrical safety standards.

Compliance Standards & Regulations

1. General Compliance
  • Valid Electrical Contractor License → Energy (Electricity Supply) Regulations, 2022
  • Compliance with Approved Design → EPRA Standards
  • Adequate Documentation of Installations → Electric Power Rules, 2006
  • Inspection & Testing Records Availability → EPRA Regulations
  • Compliance with Wiring Regulations → BS 7671 Wiring Regulations
2. Electrical System Safety
  • Properly Rated Circuit Breakers & Fuses → Kenya Electrical Code
  • Earthing & Bonding Implemented → EPRA Safety Standards
  • No Exposed Live Wires or Damaged Cables → Occupational Safety and Health Act, 2007
  • Residual Current Devices (RCDs) Installed → IEC 60364 Standard
  • Properly Labeled Electrical Panels → EPRA Safety Standards
3. Workplace Safety & Equipment Compliance
  • Inspection & Maintenance of Electrical Equipment → EPRA Standards
  • Proper Signage & Warning Labels → Occupational Safety and Health Act, 2007
  • Accessible Emergency Shut-Off Switches → IEC 60079-17
  • No Overloading of Circuits → BS 7671 Wiring Regulations
  • Emergency Safety Procedures in Place → Electricity Safety Guide
