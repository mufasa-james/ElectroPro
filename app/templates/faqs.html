Frequently Asked Questions

What is ElectroPro?

ElectroPro is a platform that connects property owners with certified electrical evaluators for safety compliance evaluations.

How do I get started?

Register an account, choose your role (Client or Evaluator), and follow the guided process to either request or perform evaluations.

What are the system requirements?

You need a modern web browser with JavaScript enabled and a stable internet connection.

How do I book an evaluation?

Log in to your account, go to the Evaluations section, and click "Book New Evaluation". Fill in the required details and submit your request.

How long does an evaluation take?

Typical evaluations take 1-2 hours, depending on the property size and complexity.

How do I pay for an evaluation?

Payments can be made through our secure payment system once an evaluator accepts your request.

How do I become an evaluator?

Register as an evaluator, submit your certifications and qualifications, and wait for approval from our team.

How do I accept evaluation requests?

Available evaluations will appear in your dashboard. Click "Accept" to take on an evaluation.

How do I submit evaluation reports?

After completing an evaluation, use the provided form to submit your findings and recommendations.

What if I encounter technical issues?

Use the "Report an Issue" feature or contact our support team for assistance.

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I update my profile?

Go to your dashboard and click on the profile settings icon to update your information.
